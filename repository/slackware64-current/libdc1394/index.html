libdc1394 (1394-Based Digital Camera Control Library)

libdc1394 is a library that provides a complete high level
application programming interface (API) for developers who wish to
control IEEE 1394 based cameras that conform to the 1394-based
Digital Camera Specifications (also known as the IIDC or DCAM
Specifications).

HOME: https://sourceforge.net/projects/libdc1394
