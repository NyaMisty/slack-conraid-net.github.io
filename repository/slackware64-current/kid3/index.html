Kid3 (Efficient Audio Tagger)

If you want to easily tag multiple MP3, Ogg/Vorbis, FLAC, 
MPC, MP4/AAC, MP2, Speex, TrueAudio, and WavPack files 
(e.g. full albums) without typing the same information again 
and again and have control over both ID3v1 and ID3v2 tags, 
then Kid3 is the program you are looking for.

HOME: http://kid3.sourceforge.net/

======================================================================
REQUIRES: id3lib libmp4v2
