aom (Open Source Video Codec)

AOMedia Video 1 (AV1) is designed to be the successor to VP9 and
also to eventually be a direct competitor for HEVC/H.265.
This Slackbuild builds the 'reference' encoder aomenc as well as
the 'reference' decoder aomdec.

HOME: https://aomedia.googlesource.com/aom
