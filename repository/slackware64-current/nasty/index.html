nasty (recover the passphrase of your GPG-key)

Nasty is a program that helps you to recover the passphrase of your
PGP or GPG-key in case you forget or lost it. It is mostly a
proof-of-concept: with a different implementation this program could
be at least 100x faster.

HOME: https://www.vanheusden.com/nasty/
