unicode-emoji (Unicode Emoji Data Files)

Emoji are pictographs (pictorial symbols) that are typically
presented in a colorful form and used inline in text. They represent
things such as faces, weather, vehicles and buildings, food and drink,
animals and plants, or icons that represent emotions, feelings, or
activities.
