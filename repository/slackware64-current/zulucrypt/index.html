zulucrypt (front end to cryptsetup and True/VeraCrypt)

zuluCrypt is a simple,feature rich and powerful solution for hard
drives encryption.
zuluCrypt does hard drives encryption and it can manage plain
dm-crypt volumes,LUKS encrypted volumes,TrueCrypt encrypted volumes
and VeraCrypt encrypted volumes.
