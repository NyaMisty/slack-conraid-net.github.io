offlineimap (Read/sync your IMAP mailboxes)

OfflineIMAP is a software to dispose your e-mail mailbox(es) as a
local Maildir. OfflineIMAP will synchronize both sides via IMAP.

HOME: http://offlineimap.org

======================================================================
REQUIRES: python-imaplib2 python-rfc6555
