fwknop (Single Packet Authorization and Port Knocking)

fwknop stands for the "FireWall KNock OPerator", and implements an
authorization scheme called Single Packet Authorization (SPA).
This method of authorization is based around a default-drop packet
filter and libpcap.

HOME: http://www.cipherdyne.org/fwknop
