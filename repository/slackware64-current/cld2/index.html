cld2 (Compact Language Detector 2)

CLD2 probabilistically detects over 80 languages in Unicode UTF-8
text, either plain text or HTML/XML. Legacy encodings must be
converted to valid UTF-8 by the caller.

HOME: https://github.com/CLD2Owners/cld2
