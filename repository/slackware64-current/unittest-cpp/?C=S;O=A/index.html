unittest-cpp (A lightweight unit testing framework for C++)

UnitTest++ is a lightweight unit testing framework for C++. It was
designed to do test-driven development on a wide variety of
platforms. Simplicity, portability, speed, and small footprint are
all very important aspects of UnitTest++.
